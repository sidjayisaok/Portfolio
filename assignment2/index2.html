About Sid Jay

A child of the eighties, Sid Jay was raised in the suburbs of New Jersey. He graduated from Rutgers University with a Bachelor of Arts degree. His previous work experience includes internships at Art Science Research Laboratory and Sterling Publishing, working as a staff writer for a North Jersey Media Group subsidairy in Passaic and Morris Counties, and a brief stint at Thomas Publishing.


His favorite topics include history, art, architectual styles, etymology, botany, technology, and running. He previously studied enough Spanish and Italian to be able to order food and prevent starvation. He also has travelled through North and Central America, including Quebec, Costa Rica and Panama.


He has a basic grasp of programming languages and concepts. He has coded successfully in Visual Basic, R, and JavaScript. His favorite coding tools are Notepad++ and Sublime Text. He also understands math concepts up to Integral Calculus and Linear Algebra. Although he works with Windows and Android daily, he is familiar with other operating systems including iOS and OS X. He tries to be browser agnostic, but prefers Firefox for his day to day web use.
